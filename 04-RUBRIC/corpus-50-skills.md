## Important caveats

- **No commit SHAs pinned.** All URLs point to `main` branch as of 2026-04-25. SKILL.md files may have changed since. The analysis applies to the version captured on the snapshot date.
- **Author concentration.** 4 authors (Anthropic, obra, Trail of Bits, michalparkola) account for 33/50 = 66% of the corpus. The "18 distinct authors" headline is true but the long tail is sparse.
- **Sampling bias toward visible repos.** Curated lists were the seed; obscure individual skills are under-represented.
- **No private skills.** The corpus is public-only by necessity.

See [`empirical-validation.md`](./empirical-validation.md) for the analysis built on this corpus, and [`../05-EVALUATION/methodology.md`](../05-EVALUATION/methodology.md) for the scoring protocol.

## How to extend this corpus (v0.2 contribution path)

To extend toward the v0.2 N=100 target, the highest-value additions are:

- **Individual practitioner skills** (long tail) — to dilute the 4-author concentration
- **Skills in non-Procedural archetypes** — Reference and Creative are under-represented
- **Skills from non-English-speaking authors** — current corpus is English-only
- **Skills from non-Claude-Code hosts** — Codex, OpenClaw, Cursor implementations would test cross-host generalizability

If you contribute a skill scored against the rubric, add the URL here in the appropriate section and submit alongside the scored evaluation file.
